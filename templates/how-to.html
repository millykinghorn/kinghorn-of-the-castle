How to use our app

1. Request your data from snapchat.

Visit the snapchat website by clicking https://accounts.snapchat.com

Log in using your account details

Select "My Data"

Submit request

2. Wait for your data to be sent to you. This normally takes up to 1 hour.

When your data arrives, click the link in the email.

Then click the download link. e.g. "mydata~29473852947.zip"

In your files, go to Downloads > mydata_29473852947.zip > "Extract All"

3. Navigate to our "Upload" page.

4. Upload the file titled "snap_history.json" or "chat_history.json"

This will now be in Downloads > mydata_29473852947 > json > snap_history

5. Choose which graph you would like to create from the drop down menu.

6. To save, right click on the graph. Then go back to return to the upload page.

Please be aware we only currently support analysis of "snap_history.json" and "chat_history.json" files but we are working to expand our capabilities.
